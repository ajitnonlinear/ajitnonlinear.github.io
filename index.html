About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Division of Dynamics, Department of Mechanical Engineering at Lodz University of Technology, Poland. My research focuses on machine learning for complex dynamical systems, extreme events, synchronization, and reservoir computing under partial observability.
