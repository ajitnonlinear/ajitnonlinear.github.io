About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Division of Dynamics, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Lodz University of Technology, Poland. My research focuses on machine learning in complex dynamical systems, with a particular emphasis on extreme events, reservoir computing, partial observability, and data-driven modeling.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at IISER Pune under Prof. Amit Apte, and a Research Associate-II at IISER Pune under Dr. Pranay Goel.

I completed my Ph.D. from NIT Sikkim under the supervision of Md Nurrujam.
